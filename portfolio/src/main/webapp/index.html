Before I get carried away with the rest of this page, I just wanted to highlight one of the organizations I'm in that has played a big role on my passion for cs.

I have always loved getting involved with the community in any way I could and Bits of Good helps by combining that with technical abilities. One of the projects I worked on was involving the creation of an online tutoring hub that links college students with students of low-income stature that may not be able to afford tutors and get the best education they can because of financial struggles. I have since transitioned to working on the operations of the club so that I can get experience in a different area while still helping others. The community within Bits of Good has also helped me to want to learn more about different technologies and is my motivation for trying unique stylings that are shown on this page.
